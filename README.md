### <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/odoo/875A7B" width="22" /> Custom Odoo modules, built from scratch

I design and develop **custom Odoo modules from scratch** — data models, views,
business logic, workflows, and reports — and I also **customize and extend
existing ones** to fit how a business actually works. From an empty module to a
fully integrated solution, I turn specific business requirements into tailored
ERP functionality.

This is what I do day to day, and what I enjoy most: building something that
didn't exist before and making it fit perfectly.

### <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/postgresql/875A7B" width="22" /> And what powers it — the data

Behind every module there's a database, and that's a part I really enjoy. I work
with **PostgreSQL** and **SQL** to model entities and design clean, well-thought
relationships — and I'm steadily growing those skills toward **data
engineering**. It's not the center of my profile today, but it's the direction
I'm building toward.
